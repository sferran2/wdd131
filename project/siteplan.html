GoalGetter

GoalGetter helps students set track, and archive their academic goals.

Site Purpose

GoalGetter, is designed to help students organize their study time, manage tasks, and track deadlines efficiently. Its purpose is to provide a structured system for students to plan their academic goals, stay on top of assignments, and improve productivity. By using GoalGetter, students can develop better time management skills, reduce stress, and stay motivated to achieve their academic success.

Scenarios

  1. How can I keep track of all my homework and exams in one place?
  2. What is the best way to manage my study time so I do not fall behind on assignments?
  3. How do I set academic goals and track my progress over time?
